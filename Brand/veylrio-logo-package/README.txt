VEYLRIO — LOGO PACKAGE
=======================================

PRIMARY LOGO
  Composition: mark + divider rule + wordmark with slogan ("Less Weight, More Momentum").
  Wordmark typeface : Sora SemiBold (outlined — no font needed to display)
  Slogan typeface   : Newsreader Italic (outlined)

PALETTES
  PRIMARY  · Teal & Terracotta
     Ink (teal)      #123A36
     Accent (clay)   #C56A4A
     Accent on dark  #E0906F
     Paper           #F0EBE1
  ALTERNATE · Espresso & Sage
     Ink (espresso)  #2A2723
     Accent (sage)   #7E9B82
     Accent on dark  #9DB8A1
     Paper           #F2ECE2

FOLDERS (per palette)
  primary-lockup/    the main logo (use this by default)
  horizontal-lockup/ mark + wordmark, no slogan — for tight spaces / headers
  stacked-lockup/    centered mark over wordmark + slogan — social avatars, hero blocks
  wordmark/          "Veylrio" on its own
  mark/              the V symbol alone, plus favicon + app-icon

TREATMENTS
  fullcolor  ink + accent (use on light/paper backgrounds)
  reversed   cream + lifted accent (use on dark backgrounds)
  mono       single ink colour (one-colour print, stamps, etch)

FORMATS
  SVG  vector — use this whenever possible (web, print). Stays sharp at any size.
  PNG  transparent raster — when SVG isn't accepted. Numbers = pixel width.
  JPG  flattened with a background — for places that reject transparency.

CLEAR SPACE & MIN SIZE
  Keep empty margin around the logo of at least the height of the "V".
  Don't display the full lockup below ~28px tall; use the horizontal lockup
  or the mark alone at small sizes.

DON'TS
  Don't recolour the mark outside the palette, stretch it, rotate it,
  add shadows/gradients, or place the fullcolor version on a busy/dark photo
  (use the reversed or mono version instead).
